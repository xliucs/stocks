Key Differentiators

🔴 Tesla FSD

  • Only vision-only at scale (removed radar/ultrasonics)
  • End-to-end neural net replaces modular pipeline
  • 3B+ miles of real-world data moat
  • Revenue: $2B+ deferred FSD revenue recognized
  • Risk: Regulatory approval for unsupervised; liability model unclear

🟢 Waymo

  • Only scaled L4 commercial service in US
  • 100k+ paid weekly rides (2x YoY growth)
  • Hardware cost declining (6th gen sensor suite cheaper)
  • Alphabet backing ($5.6B 2024 round)
  • Risk: Geofence expansion pace; unit economics at scale

🔵 China Players

  • XPeng: Most aggressive city ADAS rollout (243 cities)
  • Huawei ADS: Platform play (supplies multiple OEMs)
  • NIO: Most compute (1016 TOPS) but highest cost
  • Pony.ai: L4 robotaxi + truck; revenue generating
  • Advantage: China regulatory environment more permissive

7. Investment Thesis by Company

🔴 Tesla (TSLA) — Bullish

Bull Case ($400+)
  • FSD licensing revenue ($100B+ TAM if L4 achieved)
  • Robotaxi launch could create $500B+ revenue stream
  • Energy storage (Megapack) growing 100%+ YoY
  • Optimus robot optionality (long-term moonshot)
  • Brand & data moat: 7M+ vehicles collecting data
Bear Case ($150)
  • Auto margins compressed (price war with BYD)
  • FSD timeline repeatedly missed (2016→2026+)
  • CEO distraction (DOGE, politics polarizing brand)
  • China competition brutal; losing share to BYD
  • Valuation assumes robotaxi/robot success already

Key Catalysts: FSD unsupervised approval, robotaxi launch (Austin 2026), Model 2/Q affordable EV, Optimus pilot deployments

🟢 BYD (BABA/BYDDY) — Bullish

Bull Case
  • World's largest NEV maker by volume; unmatched vertical integration
  • Blade Battery cost leadership; in-house semiconductors
  • Global expansion: Brazil, Hungary, Thailand, Indonesia factories
  • Premium push: Denza, Fang Cheng Bao, YangWang brands
Bear Case
  • EU/US tariffs could block international expansion
  • Margin pressure from intense China price war
  • ADAS lags Tesla/XPeng in capability
  • Brand perception still weak outside China

🔵 NIO (NIO) — Neutral

Bull Case ($8+)
  • Swap station network is genuine moat (2,800+ stations)
  • Premium positioning with strong brand loyalty in China
  • ONVO sub-brand targeting mass market (L60 at ¥200k)
  • Firefly brand for compact EVs (¥70-150k)
Bear Case ($2)
  • Still burning cash heavily; dilution risk
  • Swap stations = massive capex with uncertain ROI
  • Three sub-brands stretch resources thin
  • Competition from Huawei-backed vehicles in premium segment

⚡ XPeng (XPEV) — Bullish

Bull Case ($25+)
  • Best-in-class ADAS among Chinese OEMs (XNGP city driving)
  • 800V architecture leadership (fastest charging)
  • VW partnership: platform licensing revenue stream
  • Mona brand targeting mass market, strong initial sales
Bear Case ($6)
  • Still unprofitable; path to breakeven unclear
  • ADAS investment heavy, monetization lagging
  • Competitive market makes differentiation temporary

💰 Li Auto (LI) — Bullish

Bull Case ($35+)
  • Only profitable Chinese EV startup; 20%+ gross margins
  • EREV strategy eliminates range anxiety → family SUV dominance
  • $14.5B cash war chest; no fundraising needed
  • Strong ASP ($42k+) in family SUV segment
Bear Case ($15)
  • EREV may become obsolete as charging infra improves
  • Pure EV MEGA flopped; BEV strategy uncertain
  • One-trick pony (family SUVs only)

🤖 Pony.ai (PONY) — Speculative

Bull Case
  • Leading L4 player in China; regulatory support for robotaxi
  • Robotruck revenue stream (logistics)
  • Toyota partnership for global expansion
Bear Case
  • Pre-revenue essentially; burning cash
  • Waymo/Tesla far ahead in autonomous miles
  • Geopolitical risk limits US expansion
