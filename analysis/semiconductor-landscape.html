6. Supply Chain & Geopolitical Risk

🇹🇼 TSMC Taiwan Concentration

  • ~90% of advanced chips (<7nm) made in Taiwan
  • Arizona Fab 1: 4nm production started (2025), yields improving
  • Arizona Fab 2: 3nm/2nm, operational ~2028
  • Japan Kumamoto: 12/16nm + 6nm (JASM JV with Sony/Denso)
  • Germany Dresden: 28nm (European Chips Act)
  • Cost premium: US fabs cost 2-3x more than Taiwan equivalent

🇺🇸 US-China Tech War

  • AI chip export ban: H100/H200/B200 blocked to China. NVIDIA ships cut-down H20
  • NVIDIA China revenue: Dropped from ~25% to ~12% of total
  • ASML restrictions: No EUV to China (since 2019). DUV now restricted too (2024+)
  • China response: Huawei Ascend 910B (7nm via SMIC), CXMT DRAM, domestic EDA push
  • CHIPS Act: $52.7B total. TSMC $6.6B, Intel $8.5B, Samsung $6.4B, Micron $6.1B
