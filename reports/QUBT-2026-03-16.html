Business Overview

Quantum Computing Inc. is an early-stage company focused on developing accessible quantum computing solutions and quantum photonic technologies. The company operates at the intersection of quantum hardware and software, aiming to bring quantum advantage to practical commercial applications.

  • Quantum Photonic Systems: Development of thin-film lithium niobate (TFLN) photonic chip technology for quantum and classical applications including LiDAR, telecommunications, and sensing. The company acquired QPhoton in 2022 to bolster its photonics capabilities.
  • Entropy Quantum Computing (EQC): A proprietary software platform that enables users to solve complex optimization problems by leveraging quantum computing resources, designed to be hardware-agnostic and accessible to non-quantum experts.
  • Foundry Services: Thin-film lithium niobate chip fabrication services for commercial and government customers, representing the majority of the company's nascent revenue.
Stage Warning: QUBT is a pre-revenue company with $682K in TTM revenue and a $1.64B market cap. The business model remains unproven, and commercial viability of its quantum photonic technology has not been demonstrated at scale. This is a venture-stage investment in a public-market wrapper.
