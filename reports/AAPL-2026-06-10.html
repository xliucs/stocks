🎯 Investment Thesis

AAPL is the right stock for this regime at the wrong price. In a market that suddenly grades AI on capital intensity and dilution, Apple is the structural winner — $101B of FCF after just ~$39B capex (more than MSFT+GOOG+META combined), zero raise risk, a $110B buyback — which is why it fell only −0.9% over a month that took peers down 3–9%. The bull case — 850M AI-incapable iPhones as a forced upgrade runway plus relentless Services compounding — is real but needs evidence the multiple already assumes.
