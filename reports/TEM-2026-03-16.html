Business Overview

Tempus AI, Inc. is a technology company advancing precision medicine through its proprietary AI platform and one of the world's largest libraries of clinical and molecular data. Founded by Eric Lefkofsky (Groupon co-founder), the company operates at the intersection of AI, genomics, and healthcare. Tempus collects and analyzes clinical, molecular, and imaging data to provide actionable insights for physicians, life sciences companies, and health systems. Its core products include genomic sequencing/profiling for oncology and other diseases, AI-powered clinical decision support tools, and a data licensing business that monetizes its vast multimodal dataset. The company serves over 50% of U.S. academic medical centers and partners with major pharma companies. With $1.27B in TTM revenue growing 83% YoY, Tempus is rapidly scaling but has yet to achieve profitability as it invests heavily in platform expansion and data acquisition.
