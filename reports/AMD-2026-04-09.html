  • Margin Pressure from Competition (Medium): Intense AI GPU competition could pressure margins as AMD prices aggressively to gain market share against NVIDIA.
  • Execution Risk on MI450/Helios (Medium): The MI450 and Helios launches must execute flawlessly. Any delays or performance shortfalls could damage credibility with hyperscalers.
  • Macro Headwinds (Medium): Broader economic uncertainty including potential slowdown in enterprise IT spending could affect near-term demand.
  • High Valuation Multiple (Medium): Trailing P/E of ~90x requires sustained high growth execution. Any miss on expectations could trigger multiple compression.
