🎯 Investment Thesis

LULU is a fallen-angel value setup: a once-30x-multiple compounder now at 9.1x trailing / ~10x forward earnings and 1.1x sales after a 51% one-year collapse, trading on its 52-week low with RSI at 26.5. The bear case is equally concrete and is why the multiple compressed: Americas comps are −5% to −6% with ~8 quarters of trend deterioration, Alo/Vuori/Skims are taking share, tariffs cost ~$240–320M/yr, and management just cut FY26 EPS guidance to ~$11 — so 9–10x may be fair value for a structurally maturing brand, not a bargain.

Verdict: Speculative value buy on a 12-month view — base case $132 (+18%) requires only stabilization, bull $172 (+54%) needs the O'Neill turnaround to take; size for a $88 bear tail (−21%) if US comps keep deteriorating. The Sept 3 print and the new CEO's plan are the swing factors. This is a "is it a value trap?" bet — the insider buying and cash generation tilt the risk/reward favorably, but only for investors who can hold through continued US softness.
