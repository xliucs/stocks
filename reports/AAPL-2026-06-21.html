🎯 Investment Thesis

AAPL is still the right stock for a capital-intensity-averse regime at a price that already knows it. Apple is the structural winner of the anti-capex tape — $101B FCF after just ~$39B capex, zero dilution, a ~$110B buyback — and at WWDC it doubled down on asset-light AI by renting a custom Google Gemini (~$1B/yr) to finally ship a working Siri. The bull case — the AI-incapable installed base as a forced-upgrade runway, relentless Services compounding, and the new Apple–Intel onshoring tailwind — is real but needs evidence the multiple already assumes.
