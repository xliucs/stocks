Business Overview

Apple Inc. is the world's most valuable public company ($3.68T market cap) and the dominant player in premium consumer electronics. The company designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones (iPhone), personal computers (Mac), tablets (iPad), wearables (Apple Watch, AirPods), and a growing ecosystem of services (App Store, Apple Music, iCloud, Apple TV+, Apple Pay). Apple's integrated hardware-software-services model creates deep switching costs and recurring revenue streams. With 2.2B+ active devices globally, the installed base continues to drive high-margin Services growth — now exceeding $100B annual run rate.

  • FX headwinds (SEVERITY: LOW) — ~60% international revenue exposed to dollar strength. Mitigant: Natural hedging through global cost base; pricing power absorbs modest FX impact.
