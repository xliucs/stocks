🎯 Investment Thesis

BABA is two companies: a genuinely accelerating AI/cloud platform (+38% cloud growth, AI revenue up triple-digits for 11 consecutive quarters, Qwen as China's leading open-source model family) bolted onto a barely-growing consumption business that just spent its way to a -62% non-GAAP profit decline and the first FCF-negative year in company history. At $115.38 and 12.5x forward earnings the stock looks cheap — but our own backtests show BABA has lagged QQQ by ~22 points since April on exactly this "cheap" thesis, and within its own cohort PDD and JD now trade at half the multiple. The catalyst that closes the gap is specific and dated: quick-commerce breakeven by end FY3/27 plus AI crossing 50% of cloud revenue — both 2–4 quarters away, while HFCAA overhang, a Fed-hike scare, and $91 oil suppress China ADR flows in the interim. Verdict: fundamentals justify patience, not urgency — 12-month base case $135 (+17%), with the position sized for a macro regime that is actively hostile to the trade.
