🎯 Investment Thesis

TSLA is the Mag7's pure narrative-vs-numbers trade, and the June regime shift moved the referee to the numbers' side. The narrative is genuinely advancing — full Austin-metro robotaxi coverage (June 4) after Dallas/Houston launches, FSD in 10 countries with 1.28M subscribers (+51% y/y), a fortress balance sheet ($44.7B cash) that needs no dilutive raise.
