Business Overview

Li Auto Inc. is a leading Chinese new energy vehicle (NEV) manufacturer headquartered in Beijing, specializing in premium smart electric vehicles. The company pioneered the extended-range electric vehicle (EREV) segment in China, differentiating itself from pure battery-electric competitors like NIO and XPeng by offering vehicles with both electric motors and range-extending gasoline generators — eliminating range anxiety while delivering an EV driving experience.

The product lineup centers on the L-series family SUVs (L6, L7, L8, L9) targeting the ¥200K–500K price segment — China's largest premium SUV market. In 2024, Li Auto also launched the MEGA, its first pure BEV MPV, though the model underperformed expectations. The company operates its own manufacturing facilities in Changzhou and Beijing and has built an extensive direct sales and service network across China with 400+ retail stores. Li Auto's technology platform integrates in-house developed intelligent driving (AD) and intelligent cockpit systems, positioning it at the intersection of the auto and AI industries.
