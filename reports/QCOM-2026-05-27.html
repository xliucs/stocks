🎯 Investment Thesis

QCOM is a structural re-rating story masquerading as a cyclical handset name. After +54% in one month and +91% from the 52W low, the stock has already absorbed the easy gains from Q2 FY26 (Auto +38% YoY, hyperscaler custom-silicon reveal, $20B buyback). What's not in the price: a quantified data center TAM at the June 24 Investor Day, the AI200 ramp into Q4 calendar 2026, and consensus EPS revisions higher as Auto compounds at 30%+ and the Apple modem cliff is fully sized into the model. Versus peers, QCOM trades at 21.9x forward P/E — below AVGO (23.1x), MRVL (35.9x), and well below INTC (79.1x) — with 54.8% gross margins and 22.1% op margins. The risk is real: the Apple modem business worth ~$7B/yr starts rolling off this fall; multiple expansion has outrun EPS revisions; and ~46% China revenue concentration leaves the stock exposed to any tariff re-escalation. But with a clear catalyst path (Investor Day June 24, Q3 earnings July 29 China bottoming, AI200 shipments Q4 2026) and the cheapest forward multiple among quality semis, the risk/reward into year-end is constructive.
